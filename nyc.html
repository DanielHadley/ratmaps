Where are New York City's rats seen most often? How many of them are there?

One urban legend states that there is at least one rat for every New Yorker - approximately 8.5 million. But that myth was debunked by a young statistician named Jonathan Auerbach, who used 311 call data to estimate the number of rats living in New York City.
